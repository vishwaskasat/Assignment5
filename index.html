Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite food. Here is the recipe of deliciously rich scrambled egss

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt Butter in frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and egss
  4. Use a spatula fold the eggs from edge of a pan to the center every 20 secs (as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are moist remove from the heat (it will continue cooking)
